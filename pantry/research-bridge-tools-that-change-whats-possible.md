## 2. The Core Concept — State of the Field

### What is settled

- **Constructionism produces deeper learning than passive consumption.**
- **Tools designed by teachers for their own classrooms transfer better than top-down.**
- **The shift from "saves time" to "enables possibility" is real.**

### What is disputed

- **Whether all teachers can reach this shift.** Book argues yes with discipline.
- **Whether the simulation is the right Act Three example.** TIKTOC OQ-1 names this load-bearing.

### What has changed recently (last 5 years)

- 2024–2026 agentic tools make "tools enabling new classroom experiences" more accessible.
- Brutalist three-file system is the discipline that makes ambition tractable.
